Vision

Our milestone is to make sure we have left an everlasting impact on the world to help those in need, and also to make sure we have changed the course of lives for those who were helped by us. We envision to create a world with a balance between equality and equity! We seek to take the money from the rich, and donate it to the poor and inspire others to do so as well. Although, we take money from the rich, we also compensate them with our amazing products!

Our mission is to treat the world through the eyes of robinhood. This is done through three simple steps:
